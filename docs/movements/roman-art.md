# Roman art

**Period:** c.200 BC-400 AD

## Style

- verism in portraiture (warts and all)
- narrative historical relief (Trajan's Column)
- illusionistic wall-painting (four Pompeian styles)
- atmospheric perspective in landscape
- architectural engineering (arch, vault, dome, concrete)
- copying of Greek masterworks
- triumphal arch as relief surface
- spiral continuous narrative

## Key artists

- anonymous
- Apollodorus of Damascus (architect)

## Techniques

- concrete vaulting
- true fresco
- tessera mosaic
- [[encaustic|encaustic]]
- marble relief
- bronze casting
