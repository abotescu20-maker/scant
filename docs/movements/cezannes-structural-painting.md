# Cézanne's Structural Painting

**Period:** c. 1880-1906

## Style

- treat nature in terms of the cylinder, sphere and cone
- impressionism 'made solid and durable like museum art'
- form constructed by planes of colour, not line
- multiple viewpoints tolerated for the sake of the picture's architecture — tables tilt, perspective is bent

## Key artists

- [[cezanne|Cézanne]]

## Techniques

- parallel, slightly diagonal 'constructive' brushstrokes of uniform size
- cool blue-green / warm ochre-orange modulation to push and pull planes
- outlines drawn in blue then half-erased
- distortion of ellipses and perspective in still life so apples sit right
