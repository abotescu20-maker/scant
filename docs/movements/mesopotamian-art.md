# Mesopotamian art (Sumerian / Akkadian / Assyrian)

**Period:** c.3500-500 BC

## Style

- narrative stone reliefs
- winged protective deities (lamassu)
- cuneiform and image integrated
- repetitive processions of soldiers and captives
- exaggerated musculature
- hunting and war scenes
- ziggurat architecture (stepped tower)
- glazed brick facades

## Key artists

- anonymous
- court sculptors of Nineveh

## Techniques

- stone relief carving
- glazed brick
- cylinder seal engraving
- bronze casting
- lost-wax casting
