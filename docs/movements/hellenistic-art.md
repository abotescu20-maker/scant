# Hellenistic art

**Period:** c.323-31 BC

## Style

- extreme drama and emotion (pathos)
- twisting spiral poses
- deep undercutting of drapery
- theatrical suffering (Laocoon)
- genre subjects (drunken old woman, sleeping faun)
- baroque-like movement
- landscape backgrounds in wall-painting
- virtuoso illusionism

## Key artists

- Agesander (Laocoon)
- anonymous Pergamon masters

## Techniques

- high-relief marble
- fresco (Pompeian styles)
- mosaic (Alexander mosaic)
- [[encaustic|encaustic]]
