# English landscape & portrait school (18th-19th c.)

**Period:** 1740-1850

## Style

- aristocratic full-length portrait in parkland setting (Gainsborough)
- direct outdoor oil sketching of English weather (Constable)
- watercolour as major independent medium (Turner, Girtin, Cotman)
- truth to local landscape — Stour valley, Yorkshire dales — over classical ideal

## Key artists

- Thomas Gainsborough
- Joshua Reynolds
- [[john-constable|John Constable]]
- [[jmw-turner|J.M.W. Turner]]
- Thomas Lawrence

## Techniques

- feathery high-key foliage (Gainsborough)
- plein-air oil sketches on prepared paper/millboard
- watercolour wash with reserved (unpainted) paper as highlight
- scratching out with knife/thumbnail for sparkle (Turner)
