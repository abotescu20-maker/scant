# Futurism

**Period:** 1909-1918

## Style

- Italian glorification of speed, machine, modern city, violence, war
- Marinetti's 1909 manifesto in Le Figaro
- dynamism: a galloping horse has not four legs but twenty
- simultaneity: show successive moments of motion in one image

## Key artists

- Boccioni
- Balla
- Severini
- Carrà

## Techniques

- Cubist faceting plus repeated contours / 'lines of force' streaming from moving bodies
- arc-shaped multiple exposures of legs, wheels, wings
- sculptural figures seeming to blur into the surrounding space
- diagonal compositions and strident colour
