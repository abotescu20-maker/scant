# Celtic / Hiberno-Saxon / Dark Ages art

**Period:** c.600-900 AD

## Style

- interlaced abstract patterns
- zoomorphic interlace (animal bodies stretched and knotted)
- horror vacui (fear of empty space)
- carpet pages in gospel books
- spiral and trumpet motifs
- no interest in naturalism
- stylized evangelist portraits
- jeweled metalwork aesthetic on parchment

## Key artists

- anonymous scribes (Eadfrith of Lindisfarne)

## Techniques

- manuscript illumination with vellum and mineral pigments
- cloisonné metalwork
- repoussé
- enamel
- interlace drawing
