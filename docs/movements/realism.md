# Realism

**Period:** c. 1840-1880

## Style

- paint only what you can see, no angels or gods
- dignify peasants and workers as subject matter
- rejection of academic idealization
- plein-air observation and social truth

## Key artists

- Courbet
- Millet
- Daumier
- Corot (Barbizon transition)

## Techniques

- palette-knife application of thick earth pigments (Courbet)
- sombre earth-brown and grey-green palette
- monumental scale applied to ordinary people
- lithography for satirical mass reproduction (Daumier)
