# Fauvism

**Period:** 1905-1908

## Style

- 'the wild beasts' — scandal of the 1905 Salon d'Automne
- colour liberated from descriptive duty — chosen for emotional and decorative power
- subject matter is an excuse for the colour harmony
- image flattened into an ornament

## Key artists

- [[matisse|Matisse]]
- Derain
- Vlaminck
- Rouault (overlap)

## Techniques

- violent, anti-naturalistic colour — red trees, green faces, pink skies
- broad flat patches of saturated pigment, sometimes left untouched by modelling
- drawing reduced to confident outline
- decorative arabesque of wallpaper, carpet, tablecloth treated as equal to figure
