# Baroque (Italian/Catholic)

**Period:** 1600-1750

## Style

- dramatic tenebrism — extreme light/dark contrast as spiritual revelation
- spotlight lighting against deep shadow
- diagonal dynamic compositions, figures bursting out of frame
- theatrical realism — commoners as sacred figures, dirty feet, rough hands
- Counter-Reformation emotional directness
- moment of peak action captured
- rich ochre, earth, wine-red palette
- illusionistic ceiling painting (quadratura) dissolving architecture into sky

## Key artists

- [[caravaggio|Caravaggio]]
- Annibale Carracci
- [[gian-lorenzo-bernini|Gian Lorenzo Bernini]]
- Francesco Borromini
- Pietro da Cortona
- Artemisia Gentileschi
- Guido Reni

## Techniques

- [[tenebrism|tenebrism]]
- chiaroscuro modelling with abrupt transitions
- impasto highlights on lit edges
- warm earth ground (bolus) bleeding through shadows
- sculptural modelling of drapery
- foreshortening di sotto in su (from below upward)
- marble sculpture that simulates flesh, cloud, and fabric
