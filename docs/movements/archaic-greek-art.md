# Archaic Greek art

**Period:** c.650-480 BC

## Style

- kouros (standing youth) rigid frontal pose
- Archaic smile
- geometric patterning of hair
- black-figure and red-figure vase painting
- symmetry and profile
- learning from Egyptian canon but loosening it
- idealized athletic male nude
- beginning of foreshortening on vases

## Key artists

- [[exekias|Exekias]]
- Euphronios
- Andokides
