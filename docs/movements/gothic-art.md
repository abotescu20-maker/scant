# Gothic art

**Period:** c.1150-1400 AD

## Style

- pointed arch, rib vault, flying buttress let walls become glass
- vertical soaring aspiration
- stained-glass windows as paintings of colored light
- statues freed from the column begin to move and interact
- S-curve (Gothic sway) of standing figures
- gentle smile and human tenderness (smiling angel of Reims)
- flowing naturalistic drapery over recognizable body
- scenes observed from life (ivy leaves from nature)
