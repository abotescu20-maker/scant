# Pointillism / Neo-Impressionism / Divisionism

**Period:** c. 1886-1900

## Style

- scientific, systematic extension of Impressionist broken colour
- optical mixture: dots of pure pigment fuse at viewing distance
- pre-planned, static, classical composition in contrast to Impressionist spontaneity

## Key artists

- [[seurat|Seurat]]
- Signac
- Cross

## Techniques

- tiny regular dots (points) of pure complementary colour
- geometric, frieze-like compositions with figures in profile
- colour wheels and optics theory (Chevreul, Rood) as basis
