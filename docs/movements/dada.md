# Dada

**Period:** 1916-1923

## Style

- anti-art protest born in neutral Zurich during WWI against the civilization that produced the war
- nonsense, chance, provocation as tools
- the readymade: an ordinary manufactured object designated as art destroys the cult of the hand-made masterpiece

## Key artists

- Marcel Duchamp
- Hans Arp
- Kurt Schwitters
- Francis Picabia
- Max Ernst (transition to Surrealism)

## Techniques

- readymade: urinal signed 'R. Mutt' (Fountain), bicycle wheel on stool, bottle-rack
- photomontage of cut-up press photographs
- Merz-collage from tram tickets, string, rubbish (Schwitters)
- chance: paper torn and dropped, composition wherever it lands (Arp)
