# Naive / 'Primitive' Modern Art

**Period:** c. 1890-1930

## Style

- self-taught artists admired by the avant-garde for their directness
- deliberate refusal of academic technique by trained modernists ('primitivism')

## Key artists

- Henri Rousseau 'Le Douanier'
- Utrillo
- [[chagall|Chagall]]

## Techniques

- painstaking leaf-by-leaf detail, flat space, frontal figures
- dream-jungle subjects with hidden animals
- childlike scale and perspective used deliberately
- folk-art floating figures and villages (Chagall)
