# Mannerism

**Period:** 1520-1600

## Style

- deliberate departure from High Renaissance harmony
- elongated figures with attenuated limbs and small heads
- artificial, 'mannered' elegance over naturalism
- acid, unnatural color combinations (pink, lime, acid yellow)
- compressed, ambiguous space with disorienting viewpoints
- spiritual strain and emotional intensity
- virtuoso display of difficulty (figura serpentinata)
- reaction against Raphaelesque balance

## Key artists

- Parmigianino
- Pontormo
- Bronzino
- Tintoretto
- [[el-greco|El Greco]]
- Giambologna
- Benvenuto Cellini

## Techniques

- elongation of proportions
- [[figura-serpentinata|figura serpentinata]]
- dissonant color palette
- compressed crowded compositions
- cool polished surface finish
- deliberate spatial distortion
