# Romanesque art

**Period:** c.1050-1200 AD

## Style

- massive rounded arches and thick walls
- distortion of figures to fit the architectural frame (tympanum)
- Christ in Majesty in mandorla
- hierarchic scale (Christ huge, saints smaller)
- patterned drapery with parallel grooves ('damp-fold')
- expressive not naturalistic
- visionary Last Judgement scenes
- sculpture subordinated to column and portal

## Key artists

- [[gislebertus|Gislebertus]]
- anonymous masters of Moissac, Vezelay

## Techniques

- stone relief carving
- [[buon-fresco|buon fresco]]
- champlevé enamel
- bronze casting (Renier de Huy font)
- gilt bell-metal
- [[stained-glass|stained glass]]
