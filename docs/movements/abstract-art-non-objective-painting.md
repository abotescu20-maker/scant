# Abstract Art / Non-objective Painting

**Period:** c. 1910 onward

## Style

- painting with no reference to visible world — pure form, colour, line
- Kandinsky's discovery (c. 1910) that a painting could be as moving as music without subject
- Mondrian's path from naturalistic trees to grids of black line, primary colour, white

## Key artists

- [[kandinsky|Kandinsky]]
- Mondrian
- Malevich (Suprematism)
- Kupka
- Delaunay

## Techniques

- pure geometric shapes: rectangles, circles, crosses
- Mondrian: horizontal and vertical black lines only; red, yellow, blue, white, grey; no diagonals
- Malevich Suprematism: a black square on white ground; coloured geometric shapes floating
- Kandinsky: lyrical floating biomorphic and geometric forms with musical rhythms
