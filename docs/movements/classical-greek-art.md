# Classical Greek art (High Classical)

**Period:** c.480-400 BC

## Style

- contrapposto (weight shift)
- idealized proportions
- rational harmony of parts
- the Great Awakening - naturalism of body
- foreshortening breakthrough
- drapery reveals body beneath
- balance of stillness and motion
- depiction of divine calm

- [[phidias|Phidias]]

- Polygnotos

## Techniques

- [[lost-wax-bronze-casting|lost-wax bronze casting]]

- tempera on wood panels (lost)
