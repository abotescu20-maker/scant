# Gauguin / Synthetism / Cloisonnism

**Period:** 1886-1903

## Style

- abandon Western subtlety; seek the 'primitive' and archaic
- flat areas of pure colour bounded by dark outlines
- symbolic rather than naturalistic colour
- Tahitian / Breton peasant subjects as escape from civilization

## Key artists

- [[gauguin|Gauguin]]
- Émile Bernard
- Nabis (followers: Bonnard, Vuillard)

## Techniques

- cloisonné: areas of unmodulated flat colour edged with dark contour like stained glass / medieval enamel
- simplified stylized drawing based on Breton folk art, Japanese prints, Maori carvings
- woodcut with rough crude grain left visible
- hot pinks, emerald greens, violet — colour chosen for feeling not for truth
