# Ancient Egyptian art

**Period:** c.3000 BC-30 BC

## Style

- hieratic scale (size by importance)
- frontal representation of torso
- profile head with frontal eye
- profile legs with both feet visible from inside
- rigid geometric stylization
- hieroglyphic integration with image
- registers (horizontal bands) for storytelling
- no foreshortening or perspective

## Key artists

- anonymous
- [[imhotep|Imhotep]]

## Techniques

- fresco secco on tomb walls
- relief carving (sunk and raised)
- stone sculpture (granite, basalt, limestone)
- [[encaustic|encaustic]]
- gilding
- inlay of colored stone
