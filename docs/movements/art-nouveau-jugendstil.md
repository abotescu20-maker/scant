# Art Nouveau / Jugendstil

**Period:** c. 1890-1910

## Style

- total design: architecture, furniture, posters, jewellery as one style
- flowing whiplash line taken from plants and female hair
- rejection of historical revival styles

## Key artists

- [[klimt|Klimt]]
- Mucha
- Beardsley
- Toulouse-Lautrec (poster overlap)
- Horta (architecture)
- Gaudí (architecture)

## Techniques

- sinuous S-curve / whiplash line
- flat silhouette with ornamental border
- chromolithographic posters with bold type integrated into image
- gold-leaf, mosaic, stylized peacock / iris / lily motifs
- wrought-iron tendrils in architecture
