# Late Classical Greek art

**Period:** c.400-323 BC

## Style

- softer more human gods
- gentle S-curve pose
- polished marble surface like living skin
- sfumato-like softness in shadow
- expressive emotion
- individual character replacing ideal type
- beginning of portrait art
- Apelles' renowned painted portraits (lost)

## Key artists

- [[praxiteles|Praxiteles]]
- Scopas
- Lysippos
- [[apelles|Apelles]]

## Techniques

- polished marble sculpture
- encaustic panel painting
- bronze portrait busts
