# Cretan / Minoan art

**Period:** c.2000-1400 BC

## Style

- flowing naturalistic lines
- marine and plant motifs (octopus, dolphins, lilies)
- airy wall-paintings in palaces
- slim elongated figures
- red-brown men, white-skinned women convention
- joyful worldly subjects
- no monumental temples
- bull-leaping ritual scenes

## Key artists

- anonymous Knossos painters

## Techniques

- true fresco (buon fresco on wet plaster)
- painted pottery
- faience
- gold work
