# Early Christian art

**Period:** c.200-500 AD

## Style

- simplification of Roman forms for symbolic clarity
- catacomb painting with biblical scenes
- basilica plan (longitudinal church)
- figure isolated on gold ground beginning
- abandonment of shading for flat pattern
- the story's meaning more important than beauty
- frontality returns (address the worshipper)
- gospel book illumination begins

## Key artists

- anonymous

## Techniques

- catacomb fresco
- wall and apse mosaic on gold ground
- sarcophagus relief
- illuminated manuscript
