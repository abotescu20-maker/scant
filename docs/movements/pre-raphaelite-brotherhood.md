# Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood

**Period:** 1848-1860s

## Style

- return to honesty of art 'before Raphael'
- jewel-bright colour, sharp focus in every plane
- literary, biblical, medieval subjects with moral seriousness

## Key artists

- Rossetti
- Holman Hunt
- Millais
- Burne-Jones

## Techniques

- painting on a wet white ground for luminous saturated colour
- meticulous botanical and fabric detail throughout the canvas
- no atmospheric softening — distance as sharp as foreground
