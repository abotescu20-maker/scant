# Italian Proto-Renaissance / Trecento

**Period:** c.1260-1400 AD

## Style

- Giotto's revolution: weight, volume, human feeling
- figures modelled by light and shade, not flat pattern
- foreshortening of solid bodies in believable space
- grouped figures reacting to an event
- real emotion (mourning, embracing)
- simple stage-like setting with one side open to the viewer
- fresco cycle as coherent narrative program
- Sienese sweetness and gold (Duccio, Simone Martini) balancing Florentine solidity
