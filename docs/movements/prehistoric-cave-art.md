# Prehistoric cave art

**Period:** c.15000-10000 BC

## Style

- magical/ritual purpose
- animal imagery dominant
- painted on cave walls deep underground
- strong silhouettes with contour outlines
- natural earth pigments (ochre, charcoal, manganese)
- blown or smeared pigment
- overlapping images without composition
- profile views of animals

## Key artists

- anonymous

## Techniques

- charcoal drawing
- earth pigment paint
- finger painting
- reed or bone blowing of pigment
