# Symbolism

**Period:** c. 1885-1910

## Style

- art should give visible form to ideas, dreams, moods, not record the world
- mystery, eroticism, decadence, death as themes
- decorative arabesque surface

## Key artists

- Gustave Moreau
- Odilon Redon
- Puvis de Chavannes
- Gustav Klimt
- Edvard Munch (overlap)

## Techniques

- flat decorative patterning
- dreamlike imagery, mythological figures, femmes fatales
- muted atmospheric washes (Redon) or gold-leaf ornament (Klimt)
