# Turner

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[romanticism|Romanticism]]

## Signature style

- dissolution of solid form into light, steam and mist
- whirling vortex compositions centred on a blaze of yellow-white sun
- watercolour techniques transposed to oil: thin washes, scraping, wiping
- subjects: storms at sea, burning buildings, trains in rain
