# Syd Mead

**Dates:** 1933-2019
**Movement:** [[cyberpunk|Cyberpunk]], industrial / concept design

## Signature style

- trained industrial designer (Ford Motor Company, 1959-61) who brought automotive rendering discipline to film
- signature gouache-and-airbrush paintings on illustration board, later digital
- chromed, streamlined vehicles with exaggerated perspective and reflective surfaces
- wet-street reflections and neon spill that became the *Blade Runner* look (1982)
- also designed for *Tron* (1982), *Aliens* (1986), *2010* (1984), *Mission to Mars* (2000)
- called himself a "visual futurist"

## Visual contribution

- established the canonical cyberpunk street-level frame: low angle, wet asphalt, towering ad columns
- Spinner flying car (*Blade Runner*) fused 1950s Americana with Japanese tech
- championed the idea that future design should look plausible, not alien
