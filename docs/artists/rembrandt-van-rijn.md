# Rembrandt van Rijn

**Dates:** 1606-1669
**Movement:** [[dutch-golden-age|Dutch Golden Age]]

## Signature style

- warm golden light emerging from deep umber darkness
- thick impasto on the lit side of the face; thin glaze in shadow
- scores of self-portraits tracking his own aging without flattery
- etchings with drypoint — velvety blacks from the burr
- psychological interior — a figure pausing, thinking
