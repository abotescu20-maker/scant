# Magritte

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[surrealism|Surrealism]]

## Signature style

- deadpan, flat, almost illustrational technique
- ordinary objects made strange by impossible juxtaposition or scale (apple filling a room, pipe labelled 'not a pipe')
- bowler-hatted men with obscured faces
- grey Belgian skies, realistic blues and greens
