# Richard Mosse

**Dates:** b. 1980, Kilkenny, Ireland
**Movement:** [[infrared-photography|Infrared Photography (Kodak Aerochrome)]]

## Signature style

- Irish contemporary artist who reactivated Kodak Aerochrome as a tool for conflict reportage after Kodak discontinued the film in 2009, stockpiling the remaining rolls
- *Infra* (2010–2011): large-format colour photographs of rebel-held eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo, where jungle and militants appear in saturated pink and magenta
- *The Enclave* (2013): multi-screen 16 mm infrared video installation representing Ireland at the Venice Biennale
- later projects *Incoming* (2017) and *Broken Spectre* (2022) extend the strategy to thermal imaging and multispectral camera systems covering refugee crossings and Amazon deforestation
- his work interrogates how surveillance and military optics see, and what it does to our sympathy when human suffering is rendered in a beautiful, alien palette

## Visual signatures for AI

- dense tropical foliage rendered bubblegum pink, magenta or crimson
- military figures in uniform against red hillsides; skin slightly waxy and pale green
- very large prints, extreme sharpness, deep dark skies
- staged but documentary register — portraits face the camera directly
- in *Incoming*: monochrome thermographic greyscale, glowing warm bodies against cold sea/sand
