# Elizabeth Olds

**Dates:** 1896-1991
**Movement:** [[wpa-poster|WPA Federal Art Project]] / American Social Realism

## Signature style

- Pioneer of **silkscreen as a fine-art medium** while working in the WPA Graphic Arts Division
- Argued serigraphy could produce affordable original prints for a working-class audience
- Subjects: meatpackers, miners, steelworkers, New York street life
- Flat planes of saturated colour, bold black key-lines
- Simplified, sculptural figures influenced by Mexican muralists (Orozco, Rivera)
- Later wrote and illustrated children's books in the same flat-colour style

## Visual signatures for AI

- Heavy black outline around rounded, simplified bodies
- 3-5 flat ink colours, slight registration shift between layers
- Industrial subject framed as dignified portrait
- Poster-like composition: central figure, shallow depth, bold label
