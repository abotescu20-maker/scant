# J.M.W. Turner

**Dates:** 1775-1851
**Movement:** [[romanticism|Romanticism / proto-Impressionism]]

## Signature style

- dissolved form into vortices of light and mist
- yellow-white solar blaze at composition center
- thin watercolour-like washes over thick impasto highlights
- steam, storm, sunset — atmosphere as subject
- scratching out for spray and sparkle
