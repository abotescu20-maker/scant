# Caspar David Friedrich

**Dates:** 1774-1840
**Movement:** [[romanticism|German Romanticism]]

## Signature style

- Rückenfigur — a single figure seen from behind, gazing into sublime distance
- low horizon with vast luminous sky
- ruins, dead oaks, Gothic abbeys in silvery mist
- precise tight brushwork (unlike Turner's dissolution)
- Christian-pantheist reading of landscape as revelation
