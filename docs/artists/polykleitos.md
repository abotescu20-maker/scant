# Polykleitos

**Dates:** c.450-420 BC
**Movement:** Classical Greek

## Signature style

- Theorist of the human canon. His Doryphoros (Spear-Bearer) embodied his written Canon: mathematical ratios between head, torso and limbs, balanced by chiastic contrapposto - left arm relaxed while right leg supports, right arm active while left leg trails. Muscular but harmonious, never strained.

## Key works

- Doryphoros (Spear-Bearer)
- Diadoumenos
- Canon (treatise, lost)

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Pythagorean number-harmony
**Influenced:** Lysippos, Roman copies everywhere, Renaissance proportion theorists including Leonardo
