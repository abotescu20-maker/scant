# Ben Shahn

**Dates:** 1898-1969 (born Kaunas, Lithuania; emigrated to US 1906)
**Movement:** [[wpa-poster|WPA Federal Art Project]] / Social Realism

## Signature style

- Angular, wiry figures drawn with a nervous, scratchy contour
- Social-justice subject matter: Sacco and Vanzetti series (1931-32), Depression-era workers, civil rights
- Worked in tempera, gouache, fresco (Bronx Central Annex Post Office), and lithography
- Photographer for the Farm Security Administration (1935-38)
- Flat, near-posterlike compositions with strong silhouettes
- Hand-lettered text integrated into image — later a major influence on US poster design

## Visual signatures for AI

- Thin, irregular contour line with dry-brush quality
- Muted earth palette (ochres, dusty reds, slate greys)
- Figures cropped close, often a single expressive face or pair of hands
- Hand-lettered captions in nervous calligraphic script
