# Monet

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[impressionism|Impressionism]]

## Signature style

- painted the same motif (haystacks, Rouen Cathedral, water lilies) at different times of day
- broken complementary colour, no black
- in late Giverny water-lily canvases the subject almost dissolves into coloured reflection
- worked in a floating studio-boat on the Seine
