# El Greco (Domenikos Theotokopoulos)

**Dates:** 1541-1614
**Movement:** [[mannerism|Late Mannerism / Spanish]]

## Signature style

- flame-like elongated figures with small heads
- acid cold palette: ice blue, acid lemon, cold grey-green, shot pink
- compressed airless space, clouds pressed against figures
- visionary religious ecstasy; eyes cast up, mouths parted
- hands in gestures of prayer or revelation stretching beyond natural anatomy
