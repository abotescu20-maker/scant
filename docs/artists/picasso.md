# Picasso

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[cubism|Cubism (co-inventor) and every phase after]]

## Signature style

- Blue Period (1901-04): cold blue-green monochrome, emaciated beggars
- Rose Period (1904-06): warm pinks, saltimbanques and harlequins
- Les Demoiselles d'Avignon 1907 — Iberian and African mask faces, faceted bodies — the shock that opens Cubism
- Analytic Cubism with Braque: brown-grey grid of facets
- Synthetic Cubism: collage, brighter colour, guitars and newspaper
- 1920s Neoclassical bulky bathers
- post-1925 Surrealist distortion: dislocated eyes on one side of face, mouth wrenched sideways
