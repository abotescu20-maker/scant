**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[fauvism|Fauvism]]

## Signature style

- bold pure colour chosen for decorative harmony, not likeness
- confident black contour line
- arabesques of patterned wallpaper, carpets, textiles
- late paper cut-outs: gouache-painted paper scissored into coloured shapes (Jazz, Blue Nudes)
