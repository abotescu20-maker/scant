# Jacques-Louis David

**Dates:** 1748-1825
**Movement:** [[neoclassicism|Neoclassicism]]

## Signature style

- stark Roman republican subjects (Oath of the Horatii, Death of Socrates)
- friezelike planar arrangement, figures parallel to the picture plane
- smooth polished surface — no visible brushwork
- archaeologically accurate armour, columns, sandals
- painter of the Revolution and then of Napoleon — civic-propaganda portraiture
