# Caravaggio (Michelangelo Merisi)

**Dates:** 1571-1610
**Movement:** [[baroque|Italian Baroque]]

## Signature style

- intense spotlight raking across figures in otherwise black space
- models drawn from Roman street — dirty feet, coarse hands, wrinkled faces on apostles and Madonnas
- no preparatory drawings — painted directly from the model on primed canvas
- red-brown (bolus) ground showing through half-tones
- peak-moment narrative: the exact instant of conversion, beheading, recognition
