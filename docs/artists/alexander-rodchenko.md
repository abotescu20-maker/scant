# Alexander Rodchenko

**Dates:** 1891-1956
**Movement:** [[constructivism|Russian Constructivism]]

## Signature style

- Co-founder of Constructivism; declared "pure" abstraction with three monochrome canvases in 1921 ("red, yellow and blue — it's all over")
- Pioneered **photomontage** in posters, book covers (Mayakovsky's *Pro Eto*, 1923), and the magazine *LEF*
- Radical camera angles: extreme low-angle ("worm's-eye") and overhead ("bird's-eye") shots
- Poster design for Dobrolet airline, Mosselprom, and the film *Battleship Potemkin* (1925) — bold red/black/white, diagonal type
- Designed workers' clubs, textiles, and theatre sets with [[varvara-stepanova|Stepanova]] (his wife)

## Visual signatures for AI

- Red/black/white palette
- Diagonal axis, 30-45° rotated typography
- Photomontaged face or worker inside geometric frame
- Cyrillic slab-serif or sans-serif display type
