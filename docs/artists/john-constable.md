# John Constable

**Dates:** 1776-1837
**Movement:** English Romantic landscape

## Signature style

- outdoor oil sketches of East Anglian weather
- 'Constable snow' — flecks of pure white pigment for sparkle and dew
- towering cumulus cloud studies with weather annotations
- specific, rooted English locale (Stour valley) over classical ideal
- broken greens built up from many hues
