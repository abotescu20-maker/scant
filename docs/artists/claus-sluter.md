# Claus Sluter

**Dates:** c.1340-1406
**Movement:** International Gothic / Northern sculpture

## Signature style

- Gave Gothic sculpture a new weight and realism. Prophets of the Well of Moses stand as monumental individuals with heavy wool robes, furrowed brows, real beards - the 'Burgundian realism' that anticipates Van Eyck.

## Key works

- Well of Moses, Chartreuse de Champmol, Dijon
- Portal sculpture of Champmol

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Flemish naturalism, late Gothic realism
**Influenced:** Van Eyck, 15th-c northern sculptors, Donatello (parallel developments)
