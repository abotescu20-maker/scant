# Peter Paul Rubens

**Dates:** 1577-1640
**Movement:** [[flemish-baroque|Flemish Baroque]]

## Signature style

- sweeping diagonal composition with twisting nude bodies
- warm pearly flesh with rosy tints and cool bluish shadow
- oil sketch (bozzetto) on panel as working method
- massive Antwerp workshop; pupils blocked in, master finished heads and hands
- allegory-as-politics: peace, war, marriage of kings as intertwined nudes
