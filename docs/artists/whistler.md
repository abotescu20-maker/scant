# Whistler

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** Aestheticism / art for art's sake

## Signature style

- harmonies of a few close tones: 'Nocturne in Blue and Silver', 'Arrangement in Grey and Black'
- Japanese-print-inspired flat composition
- subdued twilight palette, almost monochrome
- titles borrowed from music (Nocturne, Symphony, Harmony) signalling abstract concerns
