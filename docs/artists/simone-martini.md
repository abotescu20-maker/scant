# Simone Martini

**Dates:** c.1284-1344
**Movement:** International Gothic / Sienese

## Signature style

- Courtly elegance defined. Elongated figures, elaborate tooled gold grounds, rich textile patterns of brocade, delicate curving silhouettes. Emotion shown through subtle tilts of head and crossed glances rather than weight.

## Key works

- Annunciation (Uffizi, 1333)
- Guidoriccio da Fogliano
- Maestà (Palazzo Pubblico, Siena)

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Duccio, French Gothic illumination
**Influenced:** Avignon papal painters, International Gothic across Europe
