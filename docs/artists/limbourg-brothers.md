# Limbourg Brothers (Paul, Jean, Herman)

**Dates:** active c.1385-1416
**Movement:** International Gothic

## Signature style

- Dutch miniaturists working for the Duc de Berry. Calendar pages with jewel-like colour, ultramarine skies, meticulous observation of peasants and castle life, tiny distant landscapes, flat gold sky sometimes punched with stars - the summit of courtly manuscript painting.

## Key works

- Tres Riches Heures du Duc de Berry

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** French court illumination, Simone Martini
**Influenced:** early Flemish painting (Van Eyck), all 15th-c illumination
