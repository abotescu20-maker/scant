# Diego Velázquez

**Dates:** 1599-1660
**Movement:** [[spanish-golden-age|Spanish Golden Age]]

## Signature style

- silvery atmospheric greys for Spanish court
- loose dab-like brushwork that resolves at viewing distance
- black-on-black chromatic subtlety in court dress
- democratic dignity — jesters, dwarfs, kings treated alike
- Las Meninas-style mirror/meta-pictorial devices
