# Johannes Vermeer

**Dates:** 1632-1675
**Movement:** [[dutch-golden-age|Dutch Golden Age]]

## Signature style

- cool daylight entering from upper left through leaded window
- small pearl-like highlight dabs (pointillé) on bread crust, metal jug, pearl
- quiet figures arrested in domestic act (pouring, reading, sewing)
- yellow, ultramarine, white, lead-tin yellow dominant palette
- tiled floors used for mathematically-precise perspective
