# Cimabue

**Dates:** c.1240-1302
**Movement:** Italian Duecento / Proto-Renaissance

## Signature style

- Last great master of the Italo-Byzantine manner and teacher of Giotto. Retains gold ground and linear chrysography on drapery but begins to soften faces, give the Madonna a gentler human glance, and suggest volume under the robe.

## Key works

- Santa Trinita Madonna
- Crucifix of Santa Croce
- Assisi frescoes (partly)

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Byzantine icons, Constantinople mosaic tradition
**Influenced:** Giotto, Duccio
