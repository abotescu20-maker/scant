# Exekias

**Dates:** c.550-525 BC
**Movement:** Archaic Greek (vase painting)

## Signature style

- Master of Attic black-figure. Ajax and Achilles playing dice - figures hunched in symmetrical calm, fine incised lines cut through the black slip to reveal red clay beneath, concentrated psychological stillness rare in black-figure.

## Key works

- Ajax and Achilles Playing Dice amphora
- Dionysos in a Ship kylix

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** earlier Attic black-figure workshops
**Influenced:** red-figure pioneers like Euphronios
