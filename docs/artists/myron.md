# Myron

**Dates:** c.480-440 BC
**Movement:** Classical Greek

## Signature style

- Captured frozen moments of athletic motion. His Discobolos shows the twist at the instant before release, all tension coiled but the face serenely calm - the classical paradox of expressing violence through mathematical composure.

## Key works

- Discobolos (Discus Thrower)
- Athena and Marsyas

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** archaic bronze casting tradition
**Influenced:** Hellenistic action sculpture, all later studies of the athletic figure
