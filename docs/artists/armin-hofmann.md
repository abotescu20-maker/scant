# Armin Hofmann

**Dates:** 1920-2020
**Movement:** [[swiss-poster-international-typographic-style|Swiss Poster / International Typographic Style]]

## Signature style

- Taught at the Basel School of Design for nearly 50 years; shaped generations of graphic designers
- Posters reduced to their essential elements: one image, one word, maximal contrast
- Exploited tonal contrast between halftone photography and solid black type
- Theatre and civic posters for Basel institutions (Stadttheater, Kunsthalle)
- Author of *Graphic Design Manual* (1965) — a foundational pedagogical text
- Preference for black-and-white over colour; when colour appears it is usually a single saturated field

## Visual signatures for AI

- Black and white only, or black + one colour
- Grainy halftone photograph as central image
- Enormous headline in Akzidenz-Grotesk
- Strong figure/ground reversal
- Asymmetric, grid-anchored layout
