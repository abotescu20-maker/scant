# Nicola Pisano

**Dates:** c.1220-1284
**Movement:** Italian Proto-Renaissance (sculpture)

## Signature style

- Studied Roman sarcophagi and reintroduced classical bulk into medieval relief. His pulpit figures are crowded together in classical robes with deeply carved drapery; the Madonna borrows the grave profile of a Roman Juno.

## Key works

- Pisa Baptistery Pulpit (1260)
- Siena Cathedral Pulpit

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Roman sarcophagi, Emperor Frederick II's classicizing court
**Influenced:** Giovanni Pisano, Giotto, Renaissance sculpture
