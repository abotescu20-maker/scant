# Constable

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[romanticism|Romanticism / English landscape]]

## Signature style

- scenes of his own Suffolk countryside, canal boats, haywains
- oil sketches of clouds made outdoors — serial studies of weather
- flecks and dabs of pure white ('Constable's snow') for sparkle on leaves and water
- fresh green and silvery grey palette — no Claudian brown varnish
