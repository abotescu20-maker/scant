# Imhotep

**Dates:** c.2600 BC
**Movement:** Ancient Egyptian (Old Kingdom)

## Signature style

- First named architect in history. Designed the Step Pyramid of Djoser at Saqqara - a stacked-mastaba stone tower that translated perishable reed-and-mud architecture into precise limestone masonry. Introduced engaged columns carved like bundled papyrus and dignified regular geometry.

## Key works

- Step Pyramid of Djoser at Saqqara

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** earlier mastaba tombs, reed-bundle architecture
**Influenced:** all later Egyptian pyramid builders, Greek stone temple architects
