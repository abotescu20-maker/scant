# Manet

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[impressionism|precursor to Impressionism]]

## Signature style

- flat broad areas of colour with almost no mid-tone — shocked critics who called his nudes 'playing cards'
- black still vigorously present (unlike later Impressionists who banned black)
- modern Parisian subjects: bars, balconies, picnic, bullfight
- silvery whites, pure blacks, muted pinks
