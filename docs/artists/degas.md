# Degas

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[impressionism|Impressionism (drawn rather than painterly wing)]]

## Signature style

- strong contour and draughtsmanship — trained on Ingres
- ballet dancers, laundresses, women at the bath
- unusual croppings and high viewpoints from photography and Japanese prints
- pastel masterpieces: bold hatched colour layered dry
- bronze ballerina sculptures with real tutus
