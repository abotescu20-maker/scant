# Yves Chaland

**Dates:** 1957-1990 (French)
**Movement:** [[ligne-claire|Ligne claire]] (nouvelle ligne claire)

## Signature style

- leader of the 1980s "nouvelle ligne claire" revival that reinterpreted Hergé's idiom with ironic nostalgia
- *Freddy Lombard* series (1981-90) set in an anachronistic 1950s Europe
- *Bob Fish* (1981), *Le Jeune Albert* (1981-90) — affectionate Brussels-suburb parody
- flat colour and uniform contour preserved, but action more violent and palette smokier
- career cut short by car accident at age 33

## Visual contribution

- demonstrated ligne claire could hold darker tone without abandoning clarity
- revived atomic-age Belgian-French design iconography (cars, signage, typography)
- influenced Ted Benoit, Floc'h, and Serge Clerc in the Métal Hurlant / À Suivre circles
