# Shepard Fairey

**Dates:** b. 1970
**Movement:** [[banksy-street-art|Street Art]] / propaganda-poster revival

## Signature style

- *Obey Giant* sticker/wheatpaste campaign (begun 1989)
- Barack Obama *Hope* poster (2008) — red/beige/blue tricolour, halftoned portrait
- Heavy borrowing from [[constructivism|Constructivism]], [[wpa-poster|WPA]], and Chinese Cultural Revolution posters
- Stencil + screenprint + wheatpaste hybrid
- Flat poster colours: red, cream, navy blue, black
- Ornamental star / sun-ray / typography frames around central portrait

## Visual signatures for AI

- 2-3 colour halftone portrait
- Propaganda-poster framing with decorative border
- Bold slab or sans-serif one-word slogan
- Weathered, pasted-paper texture
