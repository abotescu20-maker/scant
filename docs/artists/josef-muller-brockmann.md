# Josef Müller-Brockmann

**Dates:** 1914-1996
**Movement:** [[swiss-poster-international-typographic-style|Swiss Poster / International Typographic Style]]

## Signature style

- Concert posters for the Zürich Tonhalle (1950s-60s) — pure geometry as visual analogue of music
- Rigorous use of the **grid system**; his book *Grid Systems in Graphic Design* (1981) codified it worldwide
- Sans-serif typography (Akzidenz-Grotesk, Helvetica), flush-left ragged right
- Black + one accent colour (red or yellow) on white
- Formal compositions based on circles, bars, and diagonals
- Co-founder of the journal *Neue Grafik* (1958)

## Visual signatures for AI

- Large field of white / negative space
- Concentric or nested circles / arcs as main motif
- Headline in Helvetica, large, upper-left quadrant
- Grid alignment perceptible even when grid is invisible
- Limited palette: black, white, red
