# Banksy

**Dates:** active c. 1990-present (identity pseudonymous)
**Movement:** [[banksy-street-art|Banksy / Street Art]]

## Signature style

- Stencil work in black spray paint on urban walls, often with one spot colour
- Social/political satire — rats, riot police, children with weapons, CCTV, monkeys
- Site-specific humour: the wall, bollard, or CCTV camera is part of the joke
- Recurring motifs: *Girl with Balloon*, *Flower Thrower*, *Kissing Coppers*, *Napalm*
- Interventions: *Dismaland* (2015), West Bank wall murals, museum "hangings"
- 2018 *Love is in the Bin* — self-shredding canvas at Sotheby's

## Visual signatures for AI

- Black monochrome stencil figures + single red/pink accent
- Weathered brick or concrete backdrop
- Drips and overspray visible at stencil edges
- Ironic subject/setting mismatch
