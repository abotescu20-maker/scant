# Gian Lorenzo Bernini

**Dates:** 1598-1680
**Movement:** [[baroque|Italian Baroque sculpture/architecture]]

## Signature style

- marble that imitates flesh pressed, cloud dissolving, silk rippling
- peak-emotion moment (Ecstasy of St Teresa, Apollo and Daphne mid-transformation)
- sculpture + architecture + light staged as Gesamtkunstwerk (bel composto)
- gilded rays of actual light falling on a marble saint
- St Peter's Square colonnade 'embracing' the pilgrim
