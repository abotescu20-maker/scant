# Francisco Goya

**Dates:** 1746-1828
**Movement:** [[rococo|bridge Rococo → Romanticism]]

## Signature style

- court portraits with unflattering psychological candor
- 'Black Paintings' from Quinta del Sordo — demons, Saturn, pilgrimages on almost-black walls
- aquatint etching (Los Caprichos, Disasters of War) — velvety grey tones with sharp bitten blacks
- crowd-as-victim compositions with anonymous executioners (The Third of May)
- satirical grotesque of folly and reason
