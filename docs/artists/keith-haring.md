# Keith Haring

**Dates:** 1958-1990
**Movement:** [[banksy-street-art|Street Art]] / post-graffiti

## Signature style

- Bold, unbroken black outlines on flat saturated colour
- "Radiant baby", barking dog, dancing figures, flying saucers
- Started with white chalk drawings on blank New York subway advertising panels (1980-85)
- Pop-Art sensibility merged with street practice; later ran Pop Shop (1986)
- Activism: AIDS awareness, anti-apartheid, anti-crack campaigns
- Rhythm lines and motion marks around figures

## Visual signatures for AI

- Thick, even-weight black contour
- Primary colours (red, yellow, blue) flat-filled
- No shading — everything reads as cartoon hieroglyph
- Figures in motion: dancing, running, jumping
