# Gislebertus

**Dates:** active c.1120-1135
**Movement:** Romanesque

## Signature style

- One of the very first sculptors of the Middle Ages to sign his work ('Gislebertus hoc fecit'). Expressive elongated figures with huge staring eyes, fluttering pleated drapery in tight parallel lines, vivid terror in the Last Judgement tympanum.

## Key works

- Last Judgement tympanum, Autun Cathedral
- Eve of Autun

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Burgundian Romanesque workshops
**Influenced:** later Romanesque carvers of central France
