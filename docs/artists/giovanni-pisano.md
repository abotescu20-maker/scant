# Giovanni Pisano

**Dates:** c.1250-1315
**Movement:** Italian Gothic (sculpture)

## Signature style

- Son of Nicola, but added Gothic emotion and movement. Figures twist, mothers clutch children, drapery flies in agitated folds; faces wrenched with sorrow or tenderness.

## Key works

- Pulpit of Sant'Andrea, Pistoia
- Pulpit of Pisa Cathedral
- Prophets of Siena Cathedral facade

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Nicola Pisano, French Gothic sculpture
**Influenced:** Tino di Camaino, late Gothic Italian sculpture
