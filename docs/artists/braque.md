# Braque

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[cubism|Cubism (co-inventor with Picasso)]]

## Signature style

- faceted landscapes at L'Estaque 1908 that gave Cubism its name ('little cubes')
- papier collé inventor (1912) — pasted wood-grain wallpaper
- muted ochre, grey, green palette
- more lyrical, less aggressive than Picasso's cubism
