# Cézanne

**Dates:** 
**Movement:** [[post-impressionism|Post-Impressionism]]

## Signature style

- constructive parallel brushstrokes of modulated colour
- Mont Sainte-Victoire painted over and over
- still lifes with tilted tables and distorted apples
- blue outlines re-drawn and re-drawn
- ochre / blue-green / rose tonality
