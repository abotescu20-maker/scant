# El Lissitzky

**Dates:** 1890-1941 (Lazar Markovich Lissitzky)
**Movement:** [[constructivism|Russian Constructivism]] / Suprematism bridge

## Signature style

- *Proun* series (1919-1927) — "Project for the Affirmation of the New" — geometric compositions bridging Suprematist painting and architecture
- Iconic propaganda poster *Beat the Whites with the Red Wedge* (1919) — pure geometry as political narrative
- Designed exhibition spaces (Pressa 1928, Cologne) with photomontage panoramas
- Book and typographic design: *For the Voice* (Mayakovsky, 1923) — movable type as pictorial element
- Bridge between Russian avant-garde and the Bauhaus / De Stijl (taught at VKhUTEMAS; collaborated with Moholy-Nagy, Schwitters)

## Visual signatures for AI

- Hard-edged red wedge, black square, white circle on white ground
- Isometric / axonometric projection
- Letterpress typography arranged as architecture
- Thin-to-thick rule lines dividing the page into compartments
