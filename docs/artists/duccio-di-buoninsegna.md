# Duccio di Buoninsegna

**Dates:** c.1255-1319
**Movement:** Sienese Gothic / Proto-Renaissance

## Signature style

- Wed Byzantine refinement to new Gothic tenderness. Gold grounds, haloes and linear gold highlights remain, but faces become delicate, melancholy, eyes elongated; drapery curves in flowing linear rhythms of sweet decorative beauty rather than Giotto's weight.

## Key works

- Maesta (Siena Cathedral, 1308-11)
- Rucellai Madonna

## Lineage

**Influenced by:** Constantinople school, Cimabue, French Gothic
**Influenced:** Simone Martini, International Gothic, all Sienese painting
