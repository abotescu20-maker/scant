# Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

**Dates:** 1780-1867
**Movement:** [[neoclassicism|Neoclassicism (late)]]

## Signature style

- pure cool contour of 'serpentine line' — deliberately elongated spines (La Grande Odalisque)
- porcelain-smooth flesh, invisible brushwork
- bourgeois portraits with shimmering silks and meticulous jewelry
- defender of drawing against Delacroix's colour
- archaic nude borrowed from Greek vases and Raphael
