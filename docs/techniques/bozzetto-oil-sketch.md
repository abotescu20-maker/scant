# bozzetto / oil sketch

**Period:** Baroque onward

## Technique

small rapid oil study on panel used to work out a large altarpiece's composition, colour, and light before scaling up.

## Visual signature

loose sketchy brushwork preserved as autonomous artworks today; shows the generative thinking of the picture.

## Practitioners

- Rubens
- Tiepolo
- Fragonard
