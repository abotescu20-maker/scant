# interlace / zoomorphic interlace

## Technique

Linear decoration in which ribbons, cords or the stretched bodies of stylized animals weave over and under each other in endlessly complex knots, filling every space with controlled ornament. Requires meticulous drafting with compass and straight-edge on vellum.

## Practitioners

- Book of Kells scribes
- Lindisfarne Gospels scribes
