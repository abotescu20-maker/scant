# gold ground with tooled halos

## Technique

Sheets of beaten gold leaf laid over red bole clay onto a panel, then burnished to mirror brightness. Haloes and decorative borders were punched and incised with metal tools to catch candlelight. The gold represented the timeless uncreated light of heaven rather than a location in space.
