# Gothic sway (S-curve)

## Technique

Standing figure pose in which the body curves like an elongated S - hip thrown out on one side, head tilted the opposite way, weight distributed so that the figure seems graceful and weightless rather than planted. The signature pose of Gothic Virgin-and-Child ivories and doorway saints.

## Practitioners

- Reims and Paris cathedral sculptors
