# chrysography (gold highlighting)

## Technique

Fine linear rays of gold drawn on drapery and hair with a brush of mordant gold or shell gold, following the folds in a schematic web. Reinforces the flat pattern of Byzantine robes and gives an abstract radiance rather than modelling volume.

## Practitioners

- Byzantine mosaicists
- [[cimabue|Cimabue]]
- early Duccio

- Byzantine
