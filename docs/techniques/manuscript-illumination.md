# manuscript illumination

## Technique

Painted decoration of books on parchment or vellum using mineral pigments (ultramarine, vermilion, lead white, verdigris) bound with gum or egg, combined with shell gold or gold leaf applied over a gesso cushion. Ranges from purely ornamental carpet pages to full miniatures.
