# camera obscura (aided drawing)

**Period:** 17th-18th c.

## Technique

optical box projecting the scene upside-down onto a paper/canvas; used to check perspective and tonal relationships, not traced mechanically.

## Visual signature

hyperreal depth of field, pinprick highlights, slightly flattened perspective characteristic of lens optics.

## Practitioners

- Vermeer (likely)
- Canaletto (for veduta)
