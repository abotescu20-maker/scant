# tenebrism

**Period:** Baroque (17th c.)

## Technique

extreme form of chiaroscuro where most of the picture is plunged into near-black darkness, with a single raking light picking out a few figures or faces, as if a spotlight had been switched on in a dark room.

## Visual signature

70-90% of canvas near-black; strongly lit faces, hands, and drapery edges against that void; hard-edged transitions with almost no mid-tones.
