# alla prima (wet-in-wet)

**Period:** 16th c. onward, peaks in Baroque & 19th c.

## Technique

painting completed in a single session into still-wet paint, without waiting for layers to dry; strokes blend by dragging rather than glazing.

## Visual signature

visible bravura brushstrokes; spontaneous, sketch-like finish even in final work.

## Practitioners

- Hals
- Velázquez (in parts)
- Manet later
