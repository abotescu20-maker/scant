# Spray paint (aerosol)

## Technique

Pigment suspended in solvent, propelled as fine atomised mist from a pressurised can. In the hands of graffiti and street artists the can becomes a drawing instrument — line weight and softness are controlled by the choice of cap (skinny cap, fat cap) and by distance from the wall.

Characteristic features:

- Soft, granular gradient at the edge of every stroke
- No absolute hard line unless used with a [[stencil-cut|stencil]]
- Overspray haze around the silhouette
- Drips when paint pools
- Tooth of brick or concrete substrate shows through unless heavily overcoated

## Practitioners

- Taki 183 and the first New York writers (late 1960s / early 1970s)
- [[keith-haring|Keith Haring]]
- [[banksy|Banksy]]
