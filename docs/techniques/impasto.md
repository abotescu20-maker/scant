# impasto

**Period:** 16th-19th c. (peaks with Rembrandt, Turner)

## Technique

pigment applied so thickly that it stands in physical relief on the canvas; palette-knife or stiff-bristle brush leaves visible ridges that catch real light.

## Visual signature

crusty raised paint on highlight passages (lace, jewels, lit cheek); physical shadow of brushstroke is part of the image.

## Practitioners

- Rembrandt
- Hals
- [[turner|Turner]]
- later van Gogh
