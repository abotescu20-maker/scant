# gold-ground mosaic

## Technique

Cubes (tesserae) of coloured glass, including glass backed with gold leaf, set irregularly into wet plaster on walls and apses. The slight tilting of each tessera and the gold reflections make the whole surface shimmer as the viewer moves or the candles flicker, producing the mystical glow of Byzantine interiors.

## Practitioners

- Ravenna mosaicists
- Hagia Sophia workshops
- Monreale and Cefalu masters
