# black-figure vase painting

## Technique

Figures drawn in silhouette with a refined clay slip onto the reddish body of an Attic vase; interior details incised with a fine needle; red and white highlights added. Firing in a controlled three-stage oxidising/reducing cycle turns the slip glossy black while the untouched clay stays red-orange.
