# aquatint etching

**Period:** late 18th c. onward

## Technique

Powdered resin dusted on the plate creates tonal areas when bitten — rich velvety blacks and grey tones as in Goya's Caprichos and Disasters of War.

## Visual signature

velvety flat greys beside sharp-bitten line, like a wash drawing; heavy atmosphere.
