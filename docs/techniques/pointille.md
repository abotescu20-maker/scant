# pointillé (Vermeer highlight dab)

**Period:** Dutch Golden Age

## Technique

small round dabs of lead-white or lead-tin yellow placed on lit edges of bread, metal, pearl — producing an aureole of light rather than a hard contour.

## Visual signature

soft glittering sparkle on highlights; edges that seem to vibrate in daylight.
