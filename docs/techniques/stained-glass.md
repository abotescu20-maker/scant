# stained glass

## Technique

Pieces of coloured (pot-metal) glass cut to shape, details painted with vitreous black or brown enamel and fired, then assembled with H-section lead came and an iron armature into a window. The image is formed by coloured light passing through, not by reflected surface - turning Gothic cathedral walls into walls of jewelled light.
