# atmospheric perspective (Roman wall-painting)

## Technique

Suggestion of distance by progressive softening and blueing of tone on remote mountains and buildings, and by diffusing their contours as if seen through intervening air. Achieved on Pompeian walls with thin tempera glazes over fresco and rediscovered only in the Renaissance.

## Practitioners

- anonymous Pompeian masters
- later Leonardo
