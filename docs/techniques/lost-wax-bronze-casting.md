# lost-wax bronze casting

## Technique

A full-size clay core is coated in wax sculpted to final surface detail, then invested in clay with runners and vents. Molten bronze is poured in, burning out the wax; the outer mould is broken off and the bronze is chased and polished. Permits hollow, large, lightweight figures impossible in solid casting.
