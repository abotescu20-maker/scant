# hieratic scale

## Technique

Figures drawn at sizes proportional to their spiritual or social importance, not to optical reality. Pharaoh towers over his slaves; Christ Pantocrator dwarfs the saints; the Virgin is bigger than the donors. Used whenever theology or power matters more than perspective.

## Practitioners

- Egyptian tomb painters
- Byzantine mosaicists
- [[gislebertus|Gislebertus]]
