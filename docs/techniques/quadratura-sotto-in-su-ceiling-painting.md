# quadratura / sotto in su ceiling painting

**Period:** Baroque

## Technique

illusionistic ceiling painting in which painted architecture continues the real architecture upward and figures are foreshortened from below so the ceiling appears to open onto heaven.

## Visual signature

extreme foreshortening of soles of feet, undersides of clouds and drapery; painted cornices merging into real ones.

## Practitioners

- Pietro da Cortona
- Andrea Pozzo
- Tiepolo
