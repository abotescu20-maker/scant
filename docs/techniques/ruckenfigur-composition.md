# Rückenfigur composition

**Period:** Romanticism

## Technique

placing a figure (or small group) with its back to the viewer, looking into the sublime landscape, so the viewer shares their contemplation.

## Visual signature

small silhouetted back on a cliff/mountain/shore; vast sky beyond; viewer identifies with the figure's gaze.
