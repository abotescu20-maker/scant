# fresco secco

## Technique

Pigments mixed with a binder (egg, glue or lime) and painted onto already-dry plaster. Less permanent than true fresco; flakes more easily but allows richer colours (including ultramarine and copper greens) that true fresco would dull or destroy.

## Practitioners

- Egyptian tomb painters
- Roman wall painters (partly)

## Associated movements

- Ancient Egyptian
- early Christian
- medieval wall-painting
