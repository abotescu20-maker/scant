# Roman concrete vaulting

## Technique

Opus caementicium - a mix of lime mortar, volcanic pozzolana sand and stone rubble - poured into wooden formwork. Let the Romans span vast interiors with barrel vaults, groin vaults and domes (Pantheon, Basilica of Maxentius) that stone post-and-lintel could never achieve.
