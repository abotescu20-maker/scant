# Egyptian canon of proportion

## Technique

Standing figures laid out on a grid - originally of 18, later 21, equal squares from sole to hairline - with fixed squares assigned to knee, waist, shoulders, etc. This fixed the shape of every royal or divine body for three thousand years and guaranteed stylistic continuity across dynasties.
