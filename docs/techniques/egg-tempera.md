# egg tempera

## Technique

Powdered pigment bound with egg yolk (sometimes plus vinegar or fig-milk) painted in small hatched strokes on a gessoed wooden panel. Dries fast, matt, jewel-bright; cannot be blended wet-in-wet, so form is built by countless parallel strokes laid side by side. Takes gold leaf beautifully.
