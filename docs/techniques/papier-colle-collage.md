# Papier collé / collage

## Technique

Pasting of ordinary real-world materials — newspaper, patterned wallpaper, oilcloth, corrugated cardboard, bus tickets — onto the picture. Invented by Braque (1912), exploded by Picasso and by Schwitters' Merz.
