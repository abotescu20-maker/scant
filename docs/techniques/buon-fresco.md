# buon fresco (true fresco)

## Technique

Pigments mixed only with water are brushed onto a freshly laid patch of wet lime plaster (the giornata). As the lime dries it chemically binds the pigment into the wall, producing brilliant matt colour that is essentially part of the stone. No corrections possible once dry - demands confident drawing.
