# tympanum relief composition

## Technique

The semicircular field above a Romanesque or Gothic church doorway filled with carved relief, usually Christ in Majesty or Last Judgement. Figures are distorted - elongated, twisted, scaled by importance - to fit the curve of the stone and communicate a single theological message to the worshipper entering below.
