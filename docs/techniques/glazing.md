# glazing

**Period:** 15th-19th c.

## Technique

transparent veil of oil colour laid over a dried under-painting; the lower layer shows through and the whole appears to glow from within.

## Visual signature

jewel-like depth of colour (especially reds, greens, blues) impossible to achieve by direct mixing; luminous shadow.

- Van Eyck
- Titian
- Rubens
- Rembrandt
