# contrapposto

## Technique

Standing pose in which the body's weight rests on one leg, throwing the hip up on that side and the shoulder down; the opposite shoulder rises and the free leg relaxes. The spine forms a gentle S. Invented in Greek Classical sculpture, it turned the rigid kouros into a figure seemingly alive and capable of movement.
